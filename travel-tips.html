Travel Safety Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while traveling:

  • Always keep your valuables secure and avoid displaying them in public.
  • Be aware of your surroundings and avoid isolated areas, especially at night.
  • Make copies of important documents such as your passport and travel insurance.
  • Familiarize yourself with local emergency numbers and health services.
  • Respect local customs and laws to avoid any legal issues.
